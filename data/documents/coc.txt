Employee Code of Conduct Policy Document

1. Overview

The Code of Conduct defines the expected standards of behavior for all employees. It ensures a respectful, ethical, and professional workplace.

--------------------------------------------------

2. Professional Behavior

2.1 Employees must:
- Maintain professionalism at all times
- Treat colleagues with respect
- Communicate clearly and respectfully

2.2 Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Verbal abuse
- Discrimination
- Unprofessional conduct

--------------------------------------------------

3. Workplace Ethics

3.1 Employees must:
- Act with integrity and honesty
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Follow company policies

3.2 Gifts and Benefits:
Employees must not accept gifts that could influence business decisions.

--------------------------------------------------

4. Anti-Harassment Policy

4.1 Zero Tolerance:
The company has a strict zero-tolerance policy for harassment.

4.2 Includes:
- Sexual harassment
- Verbal harassment
- Physical harassment

4.3 Reporting:
Employees can report incidents to HR confidentially.

--------------------------------------------------

5. Equal Opportunity Policy

5.1 The company provides equal opportunities regardless of:
- Gender
- Religion
- Caste
- Nationality

--------------------------------------------------

6. Confidentiality

6.1 Employees must:
- Protect company data
- Not share confidential information externally

6.2 Includes:
- Client data
- Internal documents
- Financial information

--------------------------------------------------

7. Use of Company Resources

7.1 Employees must:
- Use company assets responsibly
- Avoid misuse of systems and tools

7.2 Includes:
- Computers
- Internet access
- Email systems

--------------------------------------------------

8. Attendance and Punctuality

8.1 Employees must:
- Adhere to working hours
- Inform manager in case of absence

8.2 Repeated lateness may result in disciplinary action.

--------------------------------------------------

9. Social Media Policy

9.1 Employees must:
- Avoid sharing confidential company information online
- Represent the company responsibly

--------------------------------------------------

10. Disciplinary Actions

10.1 Violations may lead to:
- Warning letters
- Suspension
- Termination

--------------------------------------------------

11. Reporting Violations

11.1 Employees are encouraged to report violations.

11.2 Reports will be handled confidentially.

--------------------------------------------------

12. Compliance with Laws

12.1 Employees must follow all applicable laws and regulations.

--------------------------------------------------

End of Document
