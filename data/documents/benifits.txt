Employee Benefits Policy Document

1. Overview

The company provides a comprehensive benefits package to support employee well-being, financial security, and work-life balance.

--------------------------------------------------

2. Health Insurance

2.1 Coverage:
All employees are covered under group health insurance.

2.2 Includes:
- Hospitalization expenses
- Pre and post-hospitalization costs
- Daycare procedures

2.3 Family Coverage:
Coverage may include spouse and dependent children.

2.4 Claim Process:
Claims can be made through:
- Cashless hospital network OR
- Reimbursement process

--------------------------------------------------

3. Paid Holidays

3.1 Annual Holidays:
Employees are entitled to 12 paid public holidays per year.

3.2 Holiday List:
Published at the beginning of each year.

3.3 Festival Holidays:
Includes national and regional holidays.

--------------------------------------------------

4. Work From Home (WFH)

4.1 Eligibility:
Employees can request WFH based on:
- Role suitability
- Manager approval

4.2 Conditions:
- Must be available during working hours
- Must ensure productivity

--------------------------------------------------

5. Maternity Benefits

5.1 Leave Duration:
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of maternity leave.

5.2 Additional Support:
Flexible work arrangements may be provided post-return.

--------------------------------------------------

6. Paternity Benefits

6.1 Leave Duration:
Male employees are entitled to 5 days of paternity leave.

--------------------------------------------------

7. Employee Wellness Programs

7.1 Includes:
- Mental health support
- Counseling sessions
- Fitness programs

7.2 Access:
Programs are available through company-approved platforms.

--------------------------------------------------

8. Travel Allowance

8.1 Eligibility:
Employees required to travel for work are eligible.

8.2 Coverage:
- Transportation costs
- Accommodation expenses

8.3 Claim:
Reimbursement via HR portal with bills.

--------------------------------------------------

9. Learning and Development

9.1 Training Programs:
Employees have access to:
- Online courses
- Workshops
- Certifications

9.2 Sponsorship:
Company may sponsor job-related certifications.

--------------------------------------------------

10. Retirement Benefits

10.1 Provident Fund (PF):
Company contributes to employee PF account.

10.2 Gratuity:
Applicable after 5 years of continuous service.

--------------------------------------------------

11. Employee Discounts

11.1 Offers:
Employees may receive discounts on:
- Partner services
- Company products

--------------------------------------------------

12. Flexible Work Hours

12.1 Policy:
Flexible work hours may be allowed based on role.

12.2 Approval:
Subject to manager approval.

--------------------------------------------------

13. Insurance Beyond Health

13.1 Life Insurance:
Employees may be covered under group life insurance.

13.2 Accident Insurance:
Coverage for accidental injuries.

--------------------------------------------------

End of Document
